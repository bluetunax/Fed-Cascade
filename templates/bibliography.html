Methodology Brief

Downstream federal implementation is traditionally a black box. As you execute Activity Searches and Timeline Queries, the Fed Cascade engine continuously ingests and caches raw award descriptions locally.

This dossier illuminates that black box. It serves as a master bibliography—dynamically generated by scanning your local SQLite database to aggregate and profile all discovered Federal Sectors, Contractors/Grantees, and Target Zones organically found in the data.
